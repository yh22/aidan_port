About

SwanCity Pay is a mobile application created to bring the power of cashless payment system right into your hands, connecting online and offline merchant with the ease of quick and hassle free payment system. We are aiming to give our users the ultimate experience in advance payment system. Merchant also get to discover a new and fresh solution to their problems in managing their payment and missing cash. Merchants can manage their daily operations better and worry less about the handling of money in their businesses not to mention less trip to the bank. No more thick wallets, heavy coins, multiple cards, damaged notes, worse even losing them all. We want to promote cashless payment nations and build a massive economic corridor connecting people all around the world. Providing a simple, quick and secured payment option to our community.
With a dedicated and talented team with different backgrounds combining both professional skills and experience, we are confident to achieve our goal with great success.
