WELCOME TO DU JOUR!

Du Jour reimagines childhood favourites, both savoury and
sweet, unrestricted by geographies and boundaries.

These impressions focus on quality, taste and nostalgic
ingredients - introducing twists on classic Malaysian
memories. Set in a relaxed and welcoming social space for
group dining, meetings and date nights, Du Jour is a cafe,
restaurant and delicatessen with an engaging ambiance -
creating a unique experience for everyone who enters our
doors.

Open 6 days a week. Oh, did we mention that we are not a
French cafe?
